CPCC Cloud Support is an online, chat-based service that our college offers to students. Through CPCC Cloud Support, we can assist you in completing tasks and assignments that involve the use of Microsoft Office. Such products include Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Access. We can also answer questions you may have about our own website: www.cpcc.edu. Whether it’s registering for classes, using our Student E-mail service, or logging into Blackboard/Moodle – CPCC Cloud Support is here for you.

CPCC Cloud Support can also answer your basic questions about an assignment; unless it is a test, quiz or exam. We are happy to assist you in completing your projects but CPCC Cloud Support is not a tutoring service. If you require tutoring, please contact CPCC’s Academic Learning Center at 704-330-6474.

How to Get Support

A member of our support team will interact with you in a chat session and ask some questions to determine the nature of your problem. You can also consult our Online FAQs for answers and how to guides for common issues. If you contact us outside of our hours of operation for chat, you may choose to leave a message with our support staff. All messages will be responded to within (2) hours during normal business hours.

Chat Hours of Operation:

  • Monday - Thursday: 8:00am to 9:30pm
  • Friday: 8:00am to 5:00pm
  • Saturday: 8:00am to 12:00pm
  • Sunday: Closed

Live chat support will not be available on the following days: Martin Luther King Day, Spring Break, Easter Holiday, Memorial Day, 4th of July break, Thanksgiving Holiday and Winter Break.

How CPCC Cloud Support Works:

  • Step 1: Click on the blue Live Chat button shown below.
  • Step 2: You will then be prompted to download a small virus-free plug-in.
  • Step 3: With your permission, your support representative can view your screen and share control of your mouse and keyboard.
  • Step 4: You are in full control of your computer at all times. You can always override control of your mouse and keyboard, and you can end the screen-sharing session at any time.
