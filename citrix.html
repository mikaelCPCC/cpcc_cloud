CPCC ITS offers Citrix as our remote access offering for both students and employees. There are three methods of connecting to CPCC resources from the Citrix platform. All of them are accessible from http://remote.cpcc.edu.

  1. Virtual Desktop – is a hosted computer environment which provides access to CPCC resources from anywhere on all popular platforms.
  2. Virtual Application – is a hosted application service which provides direct access to CPCC licensed applications from anywhere on all popular platforms with the need to log into a computer.
  3. (Coming Soon) Office Computer Access – limited to full-time employees, we offer the ability to connect back to our office computer from anywhere

We recommend using Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome when using CPCC Cloud.

Please follow the instructions below:

  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Google Chrome
  • iOS
  • Android

Installing and launching the Citrix Receiver with Firefox.

1. Launch Mozilla Firefox, and enter the following URL in the address bar" http://remote.cpcc.edu. Enter your CPCC username and password and select next. Do not include any information about the domain.

2.If this is your first time using Citrix, you will be prompted to download and install the Citrix Receiver. Agree with the license agreement and click install

3.Firefox will ask you where to save the file. The default location will be your downloads folder. Click Save File

4. Once the file has completed downloading, locate the blue arrow in upper right side of the browser and click on it to open.

5. Click on the CitrixReceiver file to begin the installation process.

6. Follow the instructions presented by the installer program. It may prompt you for enabling Single Sign-on, we recommend that you do not enable this feature.

Return to top of page.

Using Your Virtual Applications.

7. After the installation will be prompted to re-enter your credentials. Re-enter them and click Log On.

8. You will be taken to a page that displays the available Desktops and Applications that you will have access to.

9. On the bottom of the screen you will see a button that lets you navigate between Apps and Desktops. Click on Apps, and a navigation pane will open on the left. Click on All Apps.

10. The All Apps menu will expand and display any apps that you can launch directly from this screen without launching a virtual desktop. Your apps may vary from the screenshot below.

11. Click on the Desktops button on the bottom of the screen. Select the desktop you would like to use. Citrix will begin to launch the virtual machine.

12. The virtual desktop will look and work exactly as a physical desktop. If this is your first time using a virtual environment, we strongly suggest taking the Getting started tour.

13. The virtual desktop will let you open programs as well as search for programs and files. In the below example, Microsoft Word was opened from the start menu.

How to Save and Access your Files

14. When saving files make sure that you are saving any files to your Doucments folder. This will usually be your default save location, but double check! Any files not saved to your Documents folder will be lost when you end your session!

15. Do not save any files to the Desktop, Download, Recent Places, etc..., folders. If you do not save in the Documents folder, your work will be lost!

16. To access your documents, simply navigate to the start button in the lower left hand corner, open the start menu and select the Documents tab. This will open all of your saved files and folders.

Return to top of page.

Logging out of CPCC Cloud

17. When logging out of the CPCC Cloud, DO NOT use the "Disconnect" method located within the Citrix pull down bar. This option allowst you to disconnect from the session, but DOES NOT log you out. Your session will remain open for 30 minutes before it is logged out automatically. Any information not saved will be lost!

18. To logout of CPCC Cloud correctly, first make sure that you save all of your work to the proper destination. Close any open programs and navigate to the start button in the bottom left hand side of the screen. Chose Log off and your CPCC Cloud session will begin to immediatly closed.

Return to top of page.

Closing the Citrix Receiver application

After your CPCC Cloud session has been closed successfully, you will be taken make to the main Citrix Reciever home page. Navigate to the upper right hand corner, select the arrow next to your name, a drop down will appear. Click on Log Off.

When the dialog You have logged off successfully. is displayed, you have logged out of the Citrix Reciever and may close the browser..
