The following instructions were written using the Google Chrome web browser. All of the information on this page is consistent with any browser that you may be using, but the screenshots and download locations may differ depending on your current browser.

We recommend using Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox when working within the CPCC Cloud, although other browsers can be used.

Installing and launching the Citrix Receiver in a web browser.

1. Launch your internet browser, and enter the following URL in the address bar: http://remote.cpcc.edu. Enter your CPCC username and password and select next.

2.If this is your first time using Citrix, you will be prompted to download and install the Citrix Receiver. Agree with the license agreement and click "Install".


Please note that there is a "Light Version" available, but we DO NOT recommend using this as it does not contain all of the features of the Standalone Receiver.

3. The Citrix Receiver will begin downloading. You can monitor it's progress in the lower left hand corner of the browser.

4. As soon as the installer has finished downloading, click on the arrow to the right and select "Open".

5. The installer will open and prepare to install the Citrix Receiver onto your system.

6. Follow the instructions presented by the installer program. It may prompt you for enabling "Single Sign-on", we recommend that you do not enable this feature.


7. After the installation simply click "Continue".

8. If the installer fails to launch, click on the "Detect again" link, and if this does not work, click on the "Already Installed" link. We DO NOT recommend using this as it does not contain all of the features of the Standalone Receiver.

9. You may prompted for permission to "Launch Application". Check the box for "Remember my choice for all links of this type" and then click on the "Launch Application" button.

Logging out of CPCC Cloud & Citrix Receiver.

1. When logging out of the CPCC Cloud, DO NOT use the "Disconnect" method located within the Citrix pull down bar. This option allows you to disconnect from the session, but DOES NOT log you out. Your session will remain open for 10 minutes before it is logged out automatically. Any information not saved will be lost!

2. To logout of CPCC Cloud correctly, first make sure that you save all of your work to the proper destination. Close any open programs and navigate to the start button in the bottom left hand side of the screen. Chose "Log off" and your CPCC Cloud session will begin to immediately closed.

3. After your CPCC Cloud session has been closed successfully, you will be taken make to the main Citrix Receiver home page. Navigate to the upper right hand corner, select the arrow next to your name, a drop down will appear. Click on "Log Off".

4. When the dialog "You have logged off successfully". is displayed, you have logged out of the Citrix Receiver and may close the browser..
