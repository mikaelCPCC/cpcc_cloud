CPCC ITS offers Citrix as our remote access offering for both students and employees. There are three methods of connecting to CPCC resources from the Citrix platform. All of them are accessible from http://remote.cpcc.edu.

  1. Virtual Desktop – is a hosted computer environment which provides access to CPCC resources from anywhere on all popular platforms.
  2. Virtual Application – is a hosted application service which provides direct access to CPCC licensed applications from anywhere on all popular platforms with the need to log into a computer.
  3. (Coming Soon) Office Computer Access – limited to full-time employees, we offer the ability to connect back to our office computer from anywhere

We recommend using Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome when using CPCC Cloud.

Installing and launching the Citrix Receiver with Firefox.

1. Launch Mozilla Firefox, and enter the following URL in the address bar: http://remote.cpcc.edu. Enter your CPCC username and password and select next.

2.If this is your first time using Citrix, you will be prompted to download and install the Citrix Receiver. Agree with the license agreement and click "Install".

3.Firefox will ask you where to save the file. The default location will be your downloads folder. Click "Save File".

4. Once the file has completed downloading, locate the blue arrow in upper right side of the browser and click on it to open.

5. Click on the Citrix Receiver file to begin the installation process.

6. Follow the instructions presented by the installer program. It may prompt you for enabling "Single Sign-on", we recommend that you do not enable this feature.

Installing and launching the Citrix Receiver with Chrome.

1. Launch Google Chrome, and enter the following URL in the address bar" http://remote.cpcc.edu. Enter your CPCC username and password and select next.

2.If this is your first time using Citrix, you will be prompted to download and install the Citrix Receiver. Agree with the license agreement and click "Install".


Please note that there is a "Light Version" available, but we DO NOT recommend using this as it does not contain all of the features of the Standalone Receiver.

3. The Citrix Receiver will begin downloading. You can monitor it's progress in the lower left hand corner of the browser.

4. As soon as the installer has finished downloading, click on the arrow to the right and select "Open".

5. The installer will open and prepare to install the Citrix Reciever onto your system.

6. Follow the instructions presented by the installer program. It may prompt you for enabling "Single Sign-on", we recommend that you do not enable this feature.


7. After the installation simply click "Continue".

8. If the installer fails to launch, click on the "Detect again" link, and if this does not work, click on the "Already Installed" link.

9. Chrome may prompt you for permission to "Launch Application". Check the box for "Remember my choice for all links of this type" and then click on the "Launch Application" button.

Installing and launching the Citrix Receiver for iOS.

1. Login to the Apple Store, search for and install the “Citrix Receiver”.

2. Launch the Citrix Receiver and select the “Add Account” button.

3. Enter "remote.cpcc.edu" in the Address text box and select Next.

4. Enter you CPCC username and password. For the domain, enter "lab.cpcc.edu ".

5. If this is the first time you have logged into the Citrix Receiver, the Citrix Receiver Favorites screen will appear blank. Select the plus button on the left side to view the available Citrix applications and desktops.

6. If this is the first time you have logged into the Citrix Receiver, the Citrix Receiver Favorites screen will appear blank. Select the plus button on the left side to view the available Citrix applications and desktops.

7. Select the icon of the Desktop or Application you would like to launch from the Citrix Receiver Favorites screen.

Using Your Virtual Applications.

1. After the installation will be prompted to re-enter your credentials. Re-enter them and click Log On.

2. You will be taken to a page that displays the available Desktops and Applications that you will have access to.

3. On the bottom of the screen you will see a button that lets you navigate between "Apps" and "Desktops". Click on "Apps", and a navigation pane will open on the left. Click on "All Apps".

4. The All Apps menu will expand and display any apps that you can launch directly from this screen without launching a virtual desktop. Your apps may vary from the screenshot below.

5. Click on the "Desktops" button on the bottom of the screen. Select the desktop you would like to use. Citrix will begin to launch the virtual machine.

6. The virtual desktop will look and work exactly as a physical desktop. If this is your first time using a virtual environment, we strongly suggest taking the Getting started tour.

7. The virtual desktop will let you open programs as well as search for programs and files. In the below example, Microsoft Word was opened from the start menu.

8. You can find all of the programs available to you by clicking on the Windows Start Button and selecting the "All Programs" option.

9. After clicking "All Programs" you will see a folder named "Microsoft Office 2013", clicking on this will expand the directory showing you all of the programs that are inside of that folder. In this example, we have chosen to open Word.

10. You can also quickly search for a program using the "Search programs and files". Type in the name of the program, and if it is available a link for it will appear in the upper left navigation menu.

11. Click on the link for Word, and Microsoft Word will open.

How to Save and Access your Files

1. When saving files make sure that you are saving any files to your Documents folder. This will usually be your default save location, but double check! Any files not saved to your Documents folder will be lost when you end your session! In this case, click on the "File" link in the ribbon.

Select "Save As".

2. A dialogue box will appear. Make sure that you are saving to your student directory and NOT to your desktop!

3. Do not save any files to the Desktop, Download, Recent Places, etc..., folders. If you do not save in the Documents folder, your work will be lost!

4. To access your documents, simply navigate to the start button in the lower left hand corner, open the start menu and select the "Documents" tab. This will open all of your saved files and folders.

5. A dialogue box will appear. Ensure that you are in your documents folder, and open your saved file.

Logging out of CPCC Cloud

1. When logging out of the CPCC Cloud, DO NOT use the "Disconnect" method located within the Citrix pull down bar. This option allows you to disconnect from the session, but DOES NOT log you out. Your session will remain open for 10 minutes before it is logged out automatically. Any information not saved will be lost!

2. To logout of CPCC Cloud correctly, first make sure that you save all of your work to the proper destination. Close any open programs and navigate to the start button in the bottom left hand side of the screen. Chose "Log off" and your CPCC Cloud session will begin to immediately closed.

Closing the Citrix Receiver application

1. After your CPCC Cloud session has been closed successfully, you will be taken make to the main Citrix Receiver home page. Navigate to the upper right hand corner, select the arrow next to your name, a drop down will appear. Click on "Log Off".

2. When the dialog "You have logged off successfully". is displayed, you have logged out of the Citrix Receiver and may close the browser..
