Use the following instructions to help you download, install, setup, and use your CPCC Cloud virtual environment. These screenshots were taken on an iPad Air 3 running iOS v9.3.1. Your device may not match the screenshots, but the steps remain the same.


Installing & launching the Citrix Receiver.

1. Login to the Apple Store, search for and install the “Citrix Receiver”.

2. Launch the Citrix Receiver and select the “Add Account” button.

3. Enter "remote.cpcc.edu" in the Address text box and select Next.

4. Enter you CPCC username and password. For the domain, enter "lab.cpcc.edu ".

5. If this is the first time you have logged into the Citrix Receiver, the Citrix Receiver Favorites screen will appear blank. Select the plus button on the left side to view the available Citrix applications and desktops.

6. If this is the first time you have logged into the Citrix Receiver, the Citrix Receiver Favorites screen will appear blank. Select the plus button on the left side to view the available Citrix applications and desktops.

7. Select the icon of the Desktop or Application you would like to launch from the Citrix Receiver Favorites screen.


Logging out of CPCC Cloud on iPad

1. When logging out of the CPCC Cloud, DO NOT use the "Disconnect" method located within the Citrix Receiver Windows Menu. This option allows you to disconnect from the session, but DOES NOT log you out. Your session will remain open for 30 minutes before it is logged out automatically. Any information not saved will be lost!

2. Click on the "peel" in the upper right hand side of the screen.

3. The Windows task bar will appear. Click on the Windows Start Button in the lower left corner.

4. The program plane will appear. Click on the "Log Off" option.

5. You will be brought back to the main Receiver page. Click on the avatar in the upper right hand corner. A dialogue box will appear. Click on the "Log Off" text to the left of "Accounts".

6. A dialogue box will appear in the middle of the screen that reads "You have logged out from all accounts.". Click OK" to close the app.
