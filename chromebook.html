Logging into CPCC Cloud on your Chromebook.

  1. To begin using the CPCC Cloud on your Chromebook, click on the "add person" option on the bottom ribbon of the login screen. Enter your CPCC student email account and select "Next".



  2. A dialogue box will appear asking you to login to your CPCC account. Fill in your CPCC Login (not your email) and password and click "Login".



  3. Your Chromebook will prompt you to re-enter you password to update the Chromebook profile. Make sure you use your student password.



  4. Your Chromebook will load your CPCC Cloud profile.

    5. Logging onto CPCC Cloud.

  5. To begin your CPCC Cloud session click on the Citrix Receiver shortcut in the bottom ribbon. The Receiver Icon should be added to the ribbon by default. If you do not see the Reciever Icon, please contact the helpdesk using this link.



  6. The Citrix Receiver application will open and ask for the Account URL. Enter https://remote.cpcc.edu and click "Connect".



  7. Another Citrix Receiver screen will appear asking you to log on. Simply enter your CPCC User Name and your Password and click "Log On".



  8. The Receiver Screen will notify that your desktop has been connected. Make sure that pop-ups are enabled in Chrome or the system will not function. If you do not know to do this, please follow "this link".



  9. Your CPCC Cloud session will being immediately.



  10. As soon as the virtual machine has connected you will be presented with a Windows 7 environment. The virtual desktop will look and work exactly as a physical desktop



    11. Working within CPCC Cloud.

  11. You can find all of the programs available to you by clicking on the Windows Start Button and selecting the "All Programs" option.



  12. After clicking "All Programs" you will see a folder named "Microsoft Office 2013", clicking on this will expand the directory showing you all of the programs that are inside of that folder. In this example, we have chosen to open Word.



  13. You can also quickly search for a program using the "Search programs and files". Type in the name of the program, and if it is available a link for it will appear in the upper left navigation menu.



  14. Click on the link for Word, and Microsoft Word will open. The look and feel as well as the functionality are exactly the same as if it were on a physical desktop.



  15. When saving files make sure that you are saving any files to your Documents folder. This will usually be your default save location, but double check! Any files not saved to your Documents folder will be lost when you end your session! In this case, click on the "File" link in the ribbon.



  16. A new options menu will display. Select the "Save As" tab.



  17. A dialogue box will appear. Make sure that you are saving to your student directory and NOT to your desktop!



  18. Do not save any files to the Desktop, Download, Recent Places, etc..., folders. If you do not save in the Documents folder, your work will be lost!



  19. To access your documents, simply navigate to the start button in the lower left hand corner, open the start menu and select the "Documents" tab. This will open all of your saved files and folders.



  20. A dialogue box will appear. Ensure that you are in your documents folder, and open your saved file.



    21. Logging out of CPCC Cloud & Citrix Receiver.

  21. When logging out of the CPCC Cloud, DO NOT use the "Disconnect" method located within the Citrix pull down bar. This option allows you to disconnect from the session, but DOES NOT log you out.

    Citrix session lengths vary depending on the program that is being used and could result in a session Log Off as early as ten to fifteen minutes. Any work not saved when the session is logged off will be lost. We recommend not leaving a session unattended, and that you save your work frequently to prevent loss of data.



  22. To logout of CPCC Cloud correctly, first make sure that you save all of your work to the proper destination. Close any open programs and navigate to the start button in the bottom left hand side of the screen. Chose "Log off" and your CPCC Cloud session will begin to immediately close.



  23. After your CPCC Cloud session has been closed successfully, you will be taken to the main Citrix Receiver home page. Navigate to the upper right hand corner, select the arrow next to your name, a drop down will appear. Click on "Log Off".



  24. When the dialog "You have logged off successfully". is displayed, you have logged out of the Citrix Receiver and may close application.
